About Me


Hey, I'm Dan!

My name is Daniel Cutz (Cutz is pronounced Kuwts). I am a designer and developer with a wide front-end skillset. I envision ideas, design concepts, and build responsive and user friendly digital experiences which are both intuitive and visually pleasing.

I am originally from Toronto. However, am now located outside of the Toronto area within the Niagara region. When I am not at my computer I am most likely biking, listening to obscure electronic music, or exploring new undiscovered lands.

Glad you found my webfolio. View my projects, take a seat, and stay a while.
